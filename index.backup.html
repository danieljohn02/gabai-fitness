Founded by Brothers

Gabai Fitness was built on a simple but powerful belief: everyone deserves elite-level personal training in an environment designed entirely around them. Founded by brothers Gilad and Tamir Gabai, our private gym in Pomona, NY is not a membership club — it's a dedicated training sanctuary serving Rockland County and the surrounding area.

Every personal training session is one hour long, fully structured, and held in a distraction-free private facility. No waiting for equipment. No background noise. No split attention. Just you, your coach, and a program built specifically for your goals.

Whether you're a beginner looking for a personal trainer near Pomona, NY or an experienced athlete chasing peak performance, our expert coaches deliver results through personalized programming, disciplined coaching, and a welcoming, supportive environment.
